## FACEBOOK GROUP POSTING TIPS

- **Check group rules first.** Many travel nurse housing groups have rules about format, required info, or how to post listings. Read the pinned posts before you post.
- **Respond to "ISO" posts within 1–2 hours.** People searching for housing move fast. Being first matters.
- **Engage before you post.** Comment helpfully on a few posts before dropping your listing. Groups respond better to members, not strangers.
- **Use a personal account, not a business page.** Facebook groups perform better with personal profiles. Post as yourself, the host.
- **Don't copy-paste the same post across all groups the same day.** Vary the template, the angle, and the day you post. Facebook's algorithm can suppress obvious copy-paste behavior.
- **Update your posts monthly.** Even a simple "Available again starting [date]" comment on your old post refreshes visibility.
- **Add 5–8 good photos.** Posts with photos significantly outperform text-only in Facebook groups. Use your best interior and exterior shots.
